orveyl.js is a 3D Non-Euclidean WebGPU Renderer,
and work-in-progress hobby project of adc -- stability not guaranteed!

To view non-interactive screenshots and demo descriptions, please visit the Gallery.


Controls:

  • WASD + Arrow keys: Move and rotate the camera. (XBox gamepad is also supported)
  • Spacebar: Place a marker.
  • Shift+Space: Remove a marker.
  • Home: Teleport to scene origin.
  • Shift+Home: Teleport to last marker.
  • Alt+Enter: Maximize the canvas. (Combine with F11 for true fullscreen!)
  • Backslash: Download the canvas as PNG.


Hyperbolic Geometry Demos:

  • NEBULA [ A | B | C | Random ]
  • POPPIES [ S | M | L | XL ]
  • FRACTAL [ Mandelbrot | Julia | Newton ]
